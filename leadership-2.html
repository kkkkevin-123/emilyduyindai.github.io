Providence Hospice & Sunrise Senior Living

At Sunrise Senior Living, I visited weekly to accompany and engage with residents, offering emotional support through music, conversation, and activities like reading stories or making bracelets. During summer breaks, I volunteered full-time, helping prepare meals, clean the dining hall, and assist staff with daily routines. These moments — from playing piano during dinner to organizing holiday events — taught me the power of kindness and connection in improving others’ well-being. At Providence Hospice, I assisted with both patient care and administrative tasks — preparing first aid and blood draw kits, archiving patient profiles, organizing medical inventories, and providing direct support and companionship to patients. This experience gave me insight into how empathy, teamwork, and careful organization work together to deliver compassionate healthcare.
