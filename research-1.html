Investigating Protein Interactions in Prostate Cancer

Project Overview

During my time at the Molecular Biology Lab at Sun Yat-sen University, I explored the complex protein interaction mechanisms underlying cancer biology. My research focused on the role of YAP1 in tumor immune evasion, a key factor in how cancer cells escape immune detection. I conducted immunoprecipitation assays to map protein binding domains and helped refine experimental protocols to improve result accuracy. This work deepened my understanding of how cellular signaling networks influence cancer progression and immune resistance. Our findings were later published in Oncogene (August 2025, DOI: 10.1038/s41388-025-03548-0), where I am listed as a co-author.
