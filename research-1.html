Investigating Protein Interactions in Prostate Cancer

Abstract

This project focused on exploring the molecular mechanisms through which YAP1 regulates immune evasion in prostate cancer. By investigating protein–protein interactions and mapping specific binding domains, we identified how YAP1-mediated signaling contributes to tumor progression and resistance to immune detection. These findings provide new insights into potential therapeutic targets for prostate cancer treatment.

Contribution

I participated in designing and conducting key experiments to study YAP1-related protein interactions. I performed immunoprecipitation assays to map protein binding domains and optimized experimental protocols to enhance accuracy and reproducibility.
