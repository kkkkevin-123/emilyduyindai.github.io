School Clubs

At Yorba Linda High School, I’ve had the opportunity to combine leadership with my passion for science and service. As the founder of the iGEM Club, I introduced synthetic and molecular biology to my school community, creating the first platform for students to explore hands-on biological research. I recruited over 35 members, organized weekly workshops, and partnered with local community colleges to secure lab access — providing students with a space to learn, collaborate, and innovate. As the Vice President of the California Scholarship Federation (CSF), I help coordinate school-wide volunteer initiatives, including beach cleanups, holiday card writing for teachers, and crafting handmade donations for the local library. Through these experiences, I’ve learned how leadership can bring people together around shared goals — whether it’s advancing science or giving back to the community.
