Developing MP6-UTT to Accelerate Microbial Adaptation (iDEC PIM-VAC)

Abstract

In this project, our team at iDEC PIM-VAC developed MP6-UTT, a genetic tool designed to accelerate microbial adaptation and enhance strain performance across diverse environments. The project aimed to improve the efficiency and robustness of engineered microbial strains for research and biotechnological applications.

Contribution

As a Wet Lab Intern and independent researcher, I conducted key experiments including plasmid extraction, molecular cloning, and adaptation assays. I helped analyze experimental data and refine protocols to improve reproducibility and efficiency. While I collaborated closely with team members and received guidance from mentors, I led several experiments and contributed significantly to the experimental design and implementation.
