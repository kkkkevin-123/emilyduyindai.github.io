JHU Summer Pre-College: Anatomy, Physiology & Disease

JHU Summer Pre-College

In summer 2023, I participated in a pre-college course taught by Dr. Ciarleglio, where I explored how different body systems interact and how biological mechanisms connect to human disease.

  • Performed dissections on a cow’s eye, sheep’s heart, liver, and lungs, tracing the optic nerve and examining real organs for the first time.
  • Studied the structure and function of the brain, learning how neurons communicate through electrical and chemical signals.
  • Investigated neurological disorders and how disruptions in neural pathways can affect behavior and body function.
  • Gained a deeper appreciation for how neuroscience bridges biology, physiology, and medicine.
