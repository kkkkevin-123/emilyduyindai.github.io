Stanford Anesthesia Summer Insitutute (SASI)

During the Stanford Anesthesia Summer Internship Program, I explored the fundamentals of anesthesia, including suturing techniques, high-quality CPR training with BLS-certified instructors, and the use of medical equipment in the operating room. I also practiced administering epidural injections and gained insight into patient care during surgical procedures. For my capstone project, I researched anosmia by interviewing patients to understand its impact on daily life and designed an essential oil diffuser prototype to enhance scent recognition and memory recall.
